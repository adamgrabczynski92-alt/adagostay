FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Do you issue VAT invoices?

Yes. We work with both private guests and business clients and issue VAT invoices.

Can I stay with a child?

Yes. Children are welcome, but they must remain under constant adult supervision at all times.

Are pets allowed?

No. Pets are not accepted.

Is parking available?

Parking is guaranteed only for selected apartments, according to the details shown in the offer.

Is early check-in or late check-out possible?

Yes, subject to prior agreement with the owner and apartment availability.

Are parties or outside visitors allowed?

No. Parties are prohibited and outside visitors are not accepted without the owner’s prior consent.

Is smoking allowed?

No. Smoking is strictly prohibited in all apartments. Breach of this rule may result in a PLN 2000 charge.

How many guests can stay?

Oaza and Gold accommodate up to 4 guests, while Antracyt accommodates up to 2 guests.
