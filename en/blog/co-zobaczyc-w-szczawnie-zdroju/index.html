What to see in Szczawno-Zdrój and nearby

A guide to a peaceful weekend: spa park, walks and comfortable accommodation.

Szczawno-Zdrój is best discovered slowly. Its spa atmosphere, park walks, cafés and relaxed pace make it an excellent choice for couples and guests looking for rest.

When planning a stay in this part of the region, it is worth choosing accommodation close to the walking area. It makes it easier to combine a slow morning, a park walk and dinner without unnecessary driving.

Oaza and Antracyt fit this kind of stay very well, offering privacy, a kitchenette, Netflix, Wi‑Fi and a comfortable standard for both short and longer trips.
