Direct booking – why is it worth it?

Faster contact, clear rules and the option to ask questions before confirmation.

Direct booking gives guests more control over the whole process. You can ask immediately about dates, guest count, parking, VAT invoice or check-in details.

It is also a faster communication path: the date enquiry form, phone and WhatsApp lead directly to the owner instead of waiting through a third-party platform.

If you want quick arrangements and a more personal approach, direct booking is usually the best option.

At the end of the day, guests care about three things: trust, accurate photos and fast communication. That is the foundation of Adago Stay.
